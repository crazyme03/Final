Tic-Tac-Toe

In Tic-Tac-Toe, you play as either the "X" player or the "O" player. The goal of the game is to have three of your symbol in a row on the board. This can be horizontally, vertically or diagonally. The "X" player moves first, and you can place your symbol by clicking the tile where you want it.
